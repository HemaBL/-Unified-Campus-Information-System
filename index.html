Unified Campus Information System (UCIS)

UCIS is a web-based platform designed to help students and staff easily locate faculty members within the campus. The system provides accurate information such as faculty cabin number, floor, and block location, also Canteen menu details and efficient.

This platform also ensures organized management of faculty details and offers secure access through role-based login.

About Us

Unified Campus Information System (UCIS) is a web-based platform designed to manage and display faculty information in a centralized way. It provides secure login and easy access to campus-related details, improving efficiency and reducing manual work.
